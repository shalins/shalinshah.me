Voice.

An app that lets blind people read

Get This App

An intelligent reader.

Printing things in Braille is expensive. As a result, so many newspaper articles and books are never printed in Braille. This tool allows blind people to listen to their books.

Simple interface

It takes 3 taps to go from opening the app to actually listening to it. Just snap a photo, click read, and that's it!

Awesome tech

Voice automatically scans the photos using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology, and starts speaking it using a TTS engine (text-to-speech).

Integrating technology with global issues

Voice is trying to use modern day technology like mobile phones and Optical Character Recognition to help with major global issues such as blindness.
